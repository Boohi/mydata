## Goal

Research, document, and decide on a viable paid Pro tier feature set and pricing model that sustains the project without compromising the open-source privacy-first mission.

## Why

The project needs a revenue model to fund ongoing development, signing certificates, and infrastructure. This must be designed carefully to avoid creating incentives that conflict with user privacy.

## Scope

In:
- Survey of comparable open-source / freemium privacy tools and their monetization.
- List of candidate Pro features (e.g., cloud sync, longer retention, team dashboards).
- Analysis of pricing models (one-time, subscription, donation).
- Decision document committed to `docs/business/pro-tier.md`.
